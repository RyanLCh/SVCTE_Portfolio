Keyboard Shortcuts

These are shortcuts commonly used on Windows, Mac, and Chromebook. Command(⌘) is used on Mac, and Ctrl is used on Windows and Chromebook. We try to use these keystrokes to avoid wrist injury from too much mousing.

Keys Function Where Used?
⌘-Z Undo All
⌘-Y Redo Editors
⌘-A Select All All
⌘-N New window/File All
⌘-F Search for wordBrowsers/Documents
⌘-C Copy All
⌘-X Cut Editors
⌘-V Paste All
⌘-R Refresh Browsers
⌘-Q Quit Browsers
⌘-S Save All
⌘-H Find & Replace Editors

Objective: Find and list 12 keyboard shortcuts.

Problem: People are experiencing wrist problems from switching to their mouse and wasting time finding what function they want to perform.

Solution: Created a clean table that lists out the shortcuts and where they are used to help them remember the keys and keep their hands on the keyboard.
